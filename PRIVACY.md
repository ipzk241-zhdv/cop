# Privacy Policy

This document outlines how the "Tic-Tac-Toe" project handles user data in accordance with GDPR requirements.

## 1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data (such as names, email addresses, IP addresses, etc.) to external servers. All gameplay and logic occur exclusively within your web browser.

## 2. Cookies and Local Storage

To ensure proper functionality and legal compliance, we use technical cookies via the `js-cookie` library.

### Cookie Usage:

- **user-cookie-consent**: This cookie stores your privacy preference. It is used to prevent the consent banner from reappearing every time the page is refreshed.

### Categories:

1.  **Necessary**: These are required for technical operations (storing consent state). They cannot be disabled as they are essential for the privacy control system's logic.
2.  **Analytics**: Currently, the project does not use third-party analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics). However, the code structure allows users to manage permissions for such services in advance.

## 3. User Rights under GDPR

Since we do not store your data, you have full control over your privacy:

- You can delete all cookies at any time through your browser settings.
- You can withdraw your consent by clearing site data, which will cause the privacy settings banner to reappear.
